Geoguessr

GeoGuessr is an interactive geography game that challenges players to guess the location of randomly placed images from Google Street View. Players are dropped into various places around the world, and using visual clues like landscapes, road signs, and buildings, they must pinpoint their exact location on a map. The closer their guess is to the actual location, the more points they earn, making it a fun and educational way to explore the world!
