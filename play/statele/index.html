Statele

Dive into Statele, the exciting word puzzle game where you test your knowledge of U.S. states! Each day, you have a set number of attempts to guess a mystery state based on its name. With each guess, you'll receive feedback on how close you are, helping you narrow down your options. Whether you're a geography buff or just looking to brush up on your state knowledge, Statele offers a fun and challenging way to learn about the diverse states of the USA. Can you figure out the mystery state before you run out of guesses?
