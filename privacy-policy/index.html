Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

At FreeGeoGames.com, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or account details. However, we collect certain types of non-personal data for the purpose of improving user experience and optimizing the website's functionality.

  • Local Storage: We use local storage to enhance your experience, such as storing game progress and settings. This data is saved locally on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to analyze website performance and serve ads.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track and analyze website traffic. Google Analytics may collect anonymous information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. This data is used to improve the functionality of our website.

    For more information on how Google Analytics collects and processes data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and preferences.

    You can learn more about how Google uses data from its partners by visiting Google's Ad Policies.

3. International Privacy Laws

We comply with the applicable privacy laws of various countries and regions, including:

For European Union (EU) Users:

In compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • You will be prompted to provide consent for the use of cookies and data tracking when you first visit the site.
  • You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at 16bitdevelopment@gmail.com.

For California Users:

In compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • California residents have the right to know what data is being collected and request that their data be deleted. While we do not collect personal data, our third-party partners (e.g., Google AdSense) may collect information through cookies and similar technologies.
  • To opt out of personalized ads, use the Do Not Sell My Personal Information link available here.

For Canadian Users:

In compliance with Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA):

  • We do not collect personal information directly, but if you wish to withdraw your consent to data tracking by third parties (e.g., Google Analytics), you can manage cookies through your browser settings.

For Australian Users:

In compliance with the Privacy Act 1988:

  • You have the right to access any personal data we may hold (though we do not collect any directly) and request corrections. Please contact us at 16bitdevelopment@gmail.com for any inquiries.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and take reasonable measures to protect it. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

  • Opting Out of Cookies: You can manage cookies through your browser settings, allowing you to block or delete cookies as you see fit.
  • Opting Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's Ad Settings at Google Ad Settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will take effect immediately.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at 16bitdevelopment@gmail.com.
